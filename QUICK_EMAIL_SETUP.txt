# Quick Email Setup Reference

## What You Need
1. EmailJS account (free)
2. Your email address (Gmail recommended)
3. Three IDs from EmailJS dashboard:
   - Public Key
   - Service ID
   - Template ID

## Where to Update
**File:** `script.js` (lines with YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY_HERE, YOUR_SERVICE_ID_HERE, YOUR_TEMPLATE_ID_HERE)

## The Three Steps
1. Create EmailJS account at emailjs.com
2. Set up Email Service (Gmail) and Email Template
3. Copy your Public Key, Service ID, and Template ID
4. Paste them into script.js

## Email Template Body
```
Name: {{from_name}}
Email: {{from_email}}
Company: {{company}}
Subject: {{subject}}

Message:
{{message}}
```

## Test After Setup
- Fill contact form
- Click Send Message
- Check your email inbox
